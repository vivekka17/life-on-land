Tree Planting

Join our community tree planting initiative to help reforest urban areas.

This event takes place every Saturday at the community park. Tools and saplings are provided for free.

Wildlife Rescue

Assist in the rescue and rehabilitation of local wildlife.

Wildlife rescue operations are held monthly. Volunteers help in animal first aid and habitat restoration.

Beach Cleanup

Participate in cleaning and restoring coastal habitats.

Cleanups are organized seasonally. Gloves, bags, and refreshments are provided to volunteers.

Eco Workshops

Engage in workshops and seminars on sustainable practices.

Workshops cover topics like recycling, composting, and renewable energy. Open to all ages.

Urban Gardening

Help transform unused spaces into thriving community gardens.

Volunteers can adopt small plots, learn about organic methods, and share produce with neighbors.

Forest Restoration

Replant native trees and remove invasive species in protected forests.

Seasonal events require moderate hiking. Training in identifying invasive species is provided.

Local Animal Shelter

Support staff by feeding, grooming, and socializing with sheltered animals.

Volunteer hours vary daily. Tasks also include cleaning kennels and assisting in adoption events.
